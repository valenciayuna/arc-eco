Core Features

Infrastructure built for real-world finance

Arc solves what existing blockchains couldn't — predictability, speed, and privacy designed for institutions from day one.

◈
USDC Native Gas

Pay transaction fees in USDC — not a speculative token. Predictable dollar-denominated costs that businesses can budget around.

Stablecoin-First
⚡
Sub-Second Finality

Deterministic finality in under one second via Malachite consensus. No reorgs, no waiting — settlement you can depend on.

Malachite BFT
◉
Opt-In Privacy

Shield transaction amounts while staying compliant. Selective disclosure via view keys — private by design, auditable when required.

TEE-Powered
⬡
Full Circle Stack

Native integration with USDC, EURC, CCTP, Circle Payments Network, and Gateway — live on Arc from day one.
